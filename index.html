our story

they deserve it

I knew there had to be a better way.

Last year, while ordering flowers for my wife aboard USS COMSTOCK the day before Mother’s Day, I thought: There has to be a better way. Gift-giving is not my specialty, and my military schedule makes it impossible to meet expectations.

We created Pelorus to give military members peace of mind while home or away. My hope is this service will let military members focus on the mission at hand, while staying in touch with all the events at home.

I want to be sure that my family and friends receive the attention they deserve while I'm deployed.

Ryan Hogan
ENS USN
Founder
